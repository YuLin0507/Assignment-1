About IVE

IVE is a six-member South Korean (Kpop) girl group under Starship Entertainment. They debuted on Dec 1, 2021 with the single album Eleven. The members of IVE are Yujin, Wonyoung, Gaeul, Liz, Rei, and Leeseo.

The group's name, IVE, comes from the English word I HAVE.
