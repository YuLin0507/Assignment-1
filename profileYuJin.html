Understand more about YUJIN

  • Her representative color is Magenta.
  • She is a former member of IZ*ONE.
  • Yujin participated in Produce 48, ranking #5, serving her a place in the final line-up.
  • She was an Inkigayo MC along with NCT‘s Sungchan and Treasure‘s Jihoon from March 7, 2021, to March 27, 2022.

  • Her shoe size is 250mm.
